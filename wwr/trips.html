Our Featured Trips

Deschutes River Half-Day Adventure

Perfect for beginners and families, this half-day trip on the Deschutes River offers a mix of Class II-III rapids and calm stretches to enjoy the stunning Central Oregon scenery. You’ll tackle rapids like “Whitehorse” and “Oak Springs” while surrounded by towering pines and basalt cliffs. This 3-hour journey includes a snack break on the riverbank and all necessary gear.

Rogue River Multi-Day Expedition

Join us for a 3-day, 2-night adventure on the Rogue River, known for its wild rapids and pristine wilderness. This trip features Class III-IV rapids like “Blossom Bar” and “Mule Creek Canyon,” plus opportunities to camp under the stars and spot wildlife such as bald eagles and deer. Meals, camping gear, and expert guides are included for an unforgettable experience.

Klamath River Full-Day Thrill Ride

Designed for adrenaline junkies, this full-day trip on the Klamath River delivers non-stop action with Class IV rapids like “Dragon’s Tooth” and “Caldera.” You’ll navigate through narrow canyons and lush forests, with a hearty riverside lunch to refuel. This 6-hour trip is ideal for experienced rafters looking for a challenge, with all gear provided.
